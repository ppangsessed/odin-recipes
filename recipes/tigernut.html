Tiger nut milk drink is an easy to make drink that is also vegan friendly. While the name does include nut, its actually a tiny root vegetable.

Ingredients

  • Peeled tiger nut
  • Water

You'll also need a cheesecloth or other alternative to strain it and a pinch of salt.

Step

  1. You start by soaking the Tiger nuts overnight with a pinch of salt. This helps to soften them and reduce anti-nutrients
  2. Rinse them and blend with fresh, filtered water
  3. Once blended, it will look frothy, creamy and a lot like almond milk. Just like homemade almond milk, you need to use a nut milk bag to strain your tiger nut milk.
  4. Pour your blended tiger nut milk through a cheesecloth and squeeze into a large bowl.
  5. Pour in desired storage container. Store in the fridge.
