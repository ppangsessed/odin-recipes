Plantain Frittata is a breakfast made of eggs and plantain slightly cooked together, usually you have to steam the eggs with fried plantain laid on top of it.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe plantains
  • 1 small tomato
  • 1/2 chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/2 chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 chopped medium onion
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Peel and fry the plantains until golden brown and set aside.
  2. In a sauce pan, heat a tbsp of oil. Saute onion until transclucent.
  3. Add the peppers and stir fry for about a minute.
  4. Season with salt and add mix with eggs in a bowl.
  5. Pour mixture into greased pan and top with the fried plantains.
  6. Cover frying pan with and lid and cook on medium heat until done.
